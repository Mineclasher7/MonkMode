Privacy Policy for Monk Mode Chrome Extension

Last Updated: 11/19/2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Monk Mode! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension. By using Monk Mode, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information:

  • Monk Mode does not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

2.2 Usage Data:

  • We may collect non-personal information about how you interact with Monk Mode, such as the frequency of use, feature preferences, and other usage statistics. This data is used for analytical purposes to enhance user experience.

3. Permissions

Monk Mode requests the following permissions for specific functionalities:

  • Host Permissions: Required to use content scripts and modify the active tab.
  • Active Tab: Required to modify the active tab and stop distractions.
  • Storage: Required to store a list of blocked URLs.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • We use the collected usage data to improve and optimize Monk Mode's features.
  • Any non-personal information is used solely for statistical purposes and is not shared with third parties.

5. Data Security

  • Monk Mode is committed to protecting your information and employs reasonable security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Monk Mode may integrate with third-party services for certain features. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes, and the updated policy will be effective upon posting.

8. Contact Us

  • For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at mineclasherdev@gmail.com.

9. Compliance

  • Monk Mode strives to comply with relevant privacy laws, including but not limited to the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), as applicable.
